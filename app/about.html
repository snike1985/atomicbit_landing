About

Atomic Bit was founded in Utah by a name not soon forgotten by the city of Salt: Zack Shutt.

With a history in radio, television, and even a brief stint working as a stuffy suit guy at Viacom in New York, Zack decided to found Atomic Bit out of necessity. Around the country and around the Salt Lake valley, the number of agencies who bring real, true value to the table is unfortunately lower than it’s ever been. When someone looks to an agency to guide them with the future of their business, it’s safe to assume it’ll take a whole lot more than a couple ping pong tables and “Wine Friday” at the agency office to strike a deal. Unfortunately, this is the case with most businesses. They get mesmerized by the agency “feel” and are in for a very expensive, very un-interesting ride.

At Atomic Bit, many of our key players don’t come from an agency background. Before you gasp, sigh, or close the window, please continue reading. We recruit intelligent, talented, business-savvy players who have experience growing and nurturing brands. After all, what good is an agency if they can’t transform your business in a way that will make you jump out of your chair and break out a bottle of High West Whiskey? It’s important. Truly. It is.

Every brand needs a story.

Many brands have one, but just don’t know how to tell it.

We’re Atomic Bit,

and we’re the story tellers you’ve been looking for.
